Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive airtime?
Airtime is typically delivered instantly after payment confirmation. In rare cases, it may take up to 5 minutes due to network congestion.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and mobile money payments.
Can I get a refund if I made a mistake?
Refunds are available for unsuccessful transactions within 24 hours. Please contact our support team for assistance.
